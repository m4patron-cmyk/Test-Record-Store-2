About the Store
Your New Independent Record Store

Located in the heart of Fort Lauderdale, Test Record Store is your destination for vinyl records and CDs spanning every genre — from classic jazz and soul to heavy metal and hip-hop. We're passionate about music and committed to keeping physical music alive.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or just discovering the warmth of vinyl, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you find your next favorite record. Stop in, flip through the crates, and stay a while.
